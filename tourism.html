Explore Popular Destinations in Rwanda

Volcanoes National Park

Home to Rwanda’s famous mountain gorillas and stunning volcanic scenery.

Nyungwe Forest

A breathtaking rainforest with rich biodiversity and scenic hiking trails.

Akagera National Park

Rwanda's savannah park offering a classic safari experience with elephants, lions, and more.

Kigali Genocide Memorial

A powerful site dedicated to honoring victims of the 1994 Rwandan Genocide.

Murambi Genocide Memorial

An important historical site for remembrance, reflecting Rwanda's commitment to peace.

Bisesero Genocide Memorial

Located in Karongi District, this site commemorates the bravery and loss of many Rwandans.

Kigali City

Rwanda’s vibrant capital, known for its cleanliness, culture, and modern skyline.

Lake Kivu

Relax by this serene lake with beautiful beaches and water activities.
